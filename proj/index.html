Commands:

H - Toggle this panel

0 - Toggle 1/4 views

1 - Front view

2 - Left view

3 - Top view

4 - Axonometric view

WhiteSpace - Toggle wireframe/solid

Q - Rotate ladder CCW

E - Rotate ladder CW

W - Raise ladder

S - Lower ladder

O - Extend ladder

P - Reduce ladder

A - Move Forward

D - Move Backward

R - Reset view params

L - Lights

I - Scale up

K - Scale down

M - Increase steps

N - Decrease steps

ArrowLeft - Decrease theta

ArrowRight - Increase theta

ArrowUp - Increase gamma

ArrowDown - Decrease gamma

MouseWheel - Zoom in/out
